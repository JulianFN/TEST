A New Planning Paradigm for Air Refueling


   AROPS Mission Planner is a software application designed to compute optimal mission profiles for both receiver and tanker aircraft when air refueling is required to complete the mission. AROPS Mission Planner also computes the optimal location for air refueling events to occur, selecting tanker aircraft from the optimal tanker base (based on available tankers as entered by the user) for the planned air refueling waypoint. The objective of the optimization is minimization of total overall Mission Cost. The receiver route is optimized by an A* route search algorithm and AROPS is able to plan air refueling events at any location (random route refueling) versus planning solely with fixed refueling tracks.
