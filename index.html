Applied Aeronautical Systems Inc.

Applied Aeronautical Systems Inc (AASI) is a Canadian based company that specializes in flight path optimization and cost management.

AASI provides a range of software products and services that enable operators of turboprop aircraft and high performance aircraft to achieve maximum flexibility at minimum cost.

Products are based on Cost Index optimization technology and are designed by pilots, thus very easy to use. Products are advisory and do not require certification or installation downtime. Return on investment time (ROI) is very short.
